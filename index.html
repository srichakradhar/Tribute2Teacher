My journey with the Art of Living

How I found my Guru

When I was a student in the Delhi University, my friends had forced me into taking the happiness program. I wanted to test how it works and had went to attend it. I liked it and started volunteering and my friends started calling me lady baba :D Nevertheless, I continued my way and after a few days I observed an evident change in their behaviour towards me, in a very good way. Then, I decided to become a teacher and I'm totally loving spreading happiness, in and around Hyderabad!
